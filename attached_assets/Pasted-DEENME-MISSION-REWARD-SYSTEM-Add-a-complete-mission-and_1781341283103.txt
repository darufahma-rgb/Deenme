3. POIN SISTEM
Each completed misi earns points:

type: 'sholat' → 20 poin
type: 'dzikir' → 10 poin
type: 'doa' → 10 poin
Completing ALL misi of one sholat → bonus 15 poin
Completing ALL misi of the day → bonus 50 poin

Store total points in state and localStorage alongside streak data.

4. LEVEL SYSTEM
Based on total cumulative points:

0-99: Mubtadi (مُبْتَدِئ)
100-299: Mutaallim (مُتَعَلِّم)
300-599: Mutawassit (مُتَوَسِّط)
600-999: Mutaqaddim (مُتَقَدِّم)
1000+: Muttaqi (مُتَّقِي)

Show level name + Arabic in the right panel of dashboard below streak card.
